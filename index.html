Matt's Tweet Poetry

An elegant mashup of the words of T.S. Eliot and the language used by Matt Clifford on twitter.

LET us go then, and I,
When the evening is spread out against the sky
Like a etherized upon a table;
Let us go, through certain streets,
The muttering retreats
Of restless in one-night cheap hotels
And sawdust restaurants with oyster-shells:
Streets that follow like a argument
Of insidious intent
To lead you to an overwhelming question….
Oh, do not ask, “What is ?”
Let us go and make our visit.
